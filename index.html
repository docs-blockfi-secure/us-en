Logging Into BlockFi: A Step-By-Step Guide

    Input your registered email address and password in the respective fields.

  3. Complete 2FA
    If 2FA is enabled, enter the code sent to your registered mobile number or authenticator app.

  4. Access Your Account
    Once verified, you’ll be redirected to your dashboard.

Using the Mobile App

  1. Download the BlockFi App
    Install the official app from your device's app store.

  2. Launch and Login
    Open the app and enter your login details.

  3. Verify Identity
    Complete 2FA for added security.

  4. Navigate the Interface

Tips for Safe Login
